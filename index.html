Culture

With three Olympic gold medals under its belt, Kosovo got all of them in judo. Kosovo won its first Olympic gold medal at the 2016 Rio Olympic Games with Majlinda Kelmendi in the women's 52kg. At Tokyo 2020, Kosovo took the women's gold with Distria Krasniqi at 48kg and Nora Gjakova at 57kg

Flag

The flag is unusual among national flags, as it uses a map of the country’s territory as a design element. This makes it one of only two countries – the other one is Cyprus – whose flag doubles as a geographic representation because it shows the silhouette of the country.

Fact

Kosovo is very open to different religions, as Albanians have three religions: Muslims, Catholics and Orthodox. There are towns in Kosovo, such as Ferizaj, where a mosque and an Orthodox church share the same courtyard.
